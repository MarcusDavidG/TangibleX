# TangibleX Demo Video Script (3-5 minutes)

## Introduction (30 seconds)

"Hello! Welcome to TangibleX - a revolutionary RealFi platform built on Mantle Network that bridges traditional finance with DeFi through compliant asset tokenization."

## Problem Statement (30 seconds)

"Traditional asset investment faces three major challenges:
1. High barriers to entry - minimum investments often exceed $100,000
2. Illiquidity - assets can take months or years to sell
3. Geographic restrictions - investors limited to their local markets"

## Platform Overview (45 seconds)

"Our platform consists of three key components:
1. Smart Contracts on Mantle Network
2. AI-Powered Analytics using GPT-4
3. Automated Oracles for real-time updates"

## Feature Demo: Asset Marketplace (60 seconds)

"Let's explore the Asset Marketplace where you can see tokenized real-world assets. Each asset shows:
- Asset type and total value
- Expected annual yield
- AI-calculated risk score
- Complete documentation on IPFS"

## Feature Demo: KYC Compliance (30 seconds)

"Regulatory compliance is built-in. Users complete our streamlined KYC process, and once verified, the smart contract grants KYC status."

## Feature Demo: Portfolio & Yield (45 seconds)

"Investors can track their holdings in real-time. Yield distribution is fully automated - yields appear automatically, proportional to your holdings."

## Technical Architecture (30 seconds)

"Under the hood, TangibleX uses:
- Solidity smart contracts with OpenZeppelin
- Mantle Network's ZK-based Layer 2
- Node.js backend with AI agents
- React frontend with Wagmi"

## Closing (20 seconds)

"TangibleX democratizes access to real-world assets on Mantle Network's cutting-edge infrastructure. Thank you for watching!"
